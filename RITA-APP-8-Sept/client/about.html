Realtime Investment & Trade Advisory (RITA)


Risk Visualization Tool leveraging IBM Watson. Risk Visualization is an application which will be used by the Advisors/Relationship Managers to assess the risk profile of their customer and suggest suitable portfolios as per their risk appetite. It also helps the investor to assess his or her risk profile independently and provides flexibility to modify risk profile based on various parameters like global economic analysis, industry performance, country performance and see the resultant impact to choice of investments as well as potential returns. It also enables users to make more informed asset allocation decisions and gives more control on financial planning, allows users to get more clarity on available investment opportunities and review what other investors of similar profile invest in. This tools provides Understanding the impact of present market scenario on portfolio, analyzing portfolio performances to be able to make informed decisions The data is received from multiple sources which needs to be aggregated and analyzed.
